Doctor Who

A website that contains the Whoniverse (It's bigger on the inside) :)

Doctor Who is a TV show about a alien (timelord) called "The Doctor" that travels around time and space with his companions. The Doctor goes around saving people, alien species, planets, galaxys, universes, dimensions, and even time itself!

These are the people who have played the Doctor:

  1. First Doctor: William Hartnell
  2. Second Doctor: Patrick Troughton
  3. Third Doctor: John Pertwee
  4. Fourth Doctor: Tom Baker
  5. Fifth Doctor: Peter Davison
  6. Sixth Doctor: Colin Baker
  7. Seventh Doctor: Sylvester McCoy
  8. Eight Doctor: Paul McGann
  9. War Doctor: John Hurt
  10. Ninth Doctor: Christopher Eccleston
  11. Tenth Doctor: David Tennant
  12. Eleventh Doctor: Matt Smith
  13. Twelth Doctor: Peter Capaldi
